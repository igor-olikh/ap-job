## About Hostaway

Hostaway is an all-in-one property management system (SaaS) for the vacation rental industry. Profitable, unicorn-status company with a 100% distributed team across 40+ countries.

## Role Summary

Lead strategy on a multi-million dollar AI business line. Shape how Hostaway AI assists, augments, and automates every aspect of property management — for managers, owners, and cleaners.

## Key Responsibilities

- Drive outcomes and own results on AI business initiatives
- Develop and implement Hostaway AI vision, strategy, and roadmap
- Collect customer and stakeholder feedback to shape features
- Create product plans with senior management based on customer interaction
- Partner with sales, customer success, and engineering for feature shipping
- Ensure proper product launches and scheduling
- Define and analyze success metrics
- Seek opportunities to innovate and expand product vision

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years of product management experience, ideally in B2B/B2C SaaS
- Strong understanding of AI/ML fundamentals to evaluate feasibility
- Understanding of LLMs including prompt design and evaluation
- Experience assessing and integrating off-the-shelf AI solutions
- Knowledge of agentic AI concepts (tool use, planning, orchestration)
- Deep empathy for users — think in flows, friction points
- Ability to make decisions amid uncertainty
- Leadership through influence without authority

## What Success Looks Like

- AI features seamlessly embedded in Hostaway product
- Customer workflows feel smarter and personalized
- AI services are stable, observable, and maintainable
- Become the go-to expert on AI product vision company-wide

## Compensation & Benefits

- Competitive salary based on applicant's country
- 100% remote (must be EMEA-based)
- Stock options in profitable, unicorn-status company
- Values-driven leadership culture
- Professional growth opportunities
- Country-specific benefits (health insurance, pensions)
- International team across 40+ countries
