# AI Product/Feature — Application Answer

As part of bringing AI into our day-to-day operations at Gameloft, I started by mapping out which routine processes were eating up the most time — time that could go toward strategic work instead.

My proposal was to use AI to improve how we monitor stability across high-load projects. Instead of the usual approach — flagging every metric deviation separately — I suggested analyzing metrics in context with related KPIs. This cuts down the noise significantly: alerts triggered by obvious, explainable reasons stop flooding the team. The ones that actually matter get verified automatically by the system, not manually by a person.

This proposal was approved and is currently being implemented. The expected result: the team will focus on making decisions based on data that's already been gathered and checked for them — faster response times, quicker decisions, and ultimately fewer customer losses because we react sooner.
