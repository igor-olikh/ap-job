# AI Product/Feature Shipped — Application Answer

At Gameloft, we had a real problem with our payments — millions of transactions across different markets, and no good way to understand why some were failing or where users were dropping off. I pushed for bringing in ML-based analytics to make sense of the transaction data. The idea was simple: instead of reacting to payment issues after the fact, let's predict which flows are likely to fail and fix them before they hurt conversion. I worked with our engineering and data teams to build this out, and it ended up giving us a much clearer picture of what to fix first. Payment success rates went up noticeably in several key markets. The biggest takeaway for me was that AI works best when you treat it as a product decision, not a tech experiment — it has to solve a real problem people actually have.
