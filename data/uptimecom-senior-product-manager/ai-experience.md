# AI / LLM Experience — Application Answer

One of the AI initiatives I proposed at Gameloft focused on improving how teams monitor stability across high-load products. The problem was that teams were receiving too many isolated alerts, which created noise and slowed down decision-making during incidents.

My approach was to rethink monitoring as contextual analysis instead of separate signal tracking. I proposed using AI to evaluate related metrics together, compare them against surrounding KPIs, and filter out alerts that were explainable or low value. The goal was to surface the issues that genuinely needed attention and reduce the amount of manual triage required from the team.

From a product perspective, this meant defining the problem in operational terms, aligning stakeholders on the expected value, and translating the idea into a practical implementation path for engineering. It also required being realistic about where AI added value: not replacing judgment, but helping teams focus on the signals most likely to affect users and revenue.

This proposal was approved and is now being implemented. The expected impact is faster response time, clearer prioritization during incidents, and better use of team capacity because data is already gathered, checked, and contextualized before someone needs to act on it.
