## About Uptime.com

Uptime.com provides real-time monitoring and observability for websites, APIs, and critical infrastructure. Engineering and operations teams use Uptime to detect outages, diagnose performance issues, and keep services available worldwide.

## About the Role

As Senior Product Manager, you will own product requirements and work closely with Engineering and Design to drive products from discovery through launch. You will also partner with Sales, Marketing, and Customer Success to support adoption and continuous improvement.

Uptime is looking for a strategic B2B SaaS product leader who can shape the roadmap, analyze the competitive landscape, and identify opportunities to build best-in-class solutions. Experience using AI agents and LLMs in product development is strongly preferred.

## Who They're Looking For

- **Entrepreneurial mindset:** Drive product vision, identify market opportunities, and take ownership of outcomes and metrics
- **AI and LLM fluency:** Understand how AI and LLMs are changing product development and SaaS capabilities, and apply these tools in daily work
- **Execution excellence:** Deliver high-impact products across the full product lifecycle while working with multiple engineering teams
- **Operational leadership:** Establish and scale strong product management practices across discovery, roadmapping, prioritization, and delivery
- **Professional collaboration:** Work effectively with Engineering, Design, Leadership, customers, and vendors
